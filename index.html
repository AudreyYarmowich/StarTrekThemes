SF Themes in Star Trek

Welcome

With 774 episodes across 6 decades of writing, 13 feature length films, and with 43 episodes across 4 seperate series set to air later this year, no series has encompassed more of the sci-fi genre than Star Trek. The universe of the Federation and Klingons has enspired generations with tales of exploration and adventure and has stood as a foundation of the science fiction community for over 54 years.

Objective

The primary objective of this site is to anaylse various subgenres of "SF" science fiction and how Star Trek tackled these topics and how they evolved across the decades
